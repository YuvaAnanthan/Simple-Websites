About Us

Founded in 2015, Karupatti Coffee is a cozy place where tradition meets taste. We bring the finest Indian brews and snacks to your table, crafted with passion and served with love.

Our journey began in the small streets of Kumbakonam, where we mastered the art of traditional coffee making. Today, we continue to honor those traditions while innovating with new flavors.
